The Best Movies and Tv shows According to Amin

My top 3 movies of all-time

Training Day

Intense and gripping, Denzel Washington delivers a powerhouse performance in this gritty exploration of corruption in law enforcement.

The Departed

A masterclass in tension and deception, Scorsese's crime epic is a riveting rollercoaster ride with stellar performances from an all-star cast.

Friday

Hilarious and timeless, this cult classic comedy offers a laid-back yet uproarious look at a day in the life of two friends in South Central LA.

My top 3 tv shows of all-time

Breaking Bad

A groundbreaking masterpiece of television, Breaking Bad mesmerizes with its gripping storytelling, complex characters, and Bryan Cranston's unforgettable transformation into the iconic antihero, Walter White.

How to Get Away with Murder

A thrilling legal drama packed with twists and turns, Viola Davis commands the screen in this suspenseful series that keeps viewers guessing until the very end.

Peaky Blinders

Stylish, gritty, and utterly compelling, Peaky Blinders captivates with its atmospheric period setting, stellar performances, and intricate tale of a notorious crime family's rise to power in post-WWI Birmingham.
